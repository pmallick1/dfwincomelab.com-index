Every day, all over the country, teachers are helping their students learn democracy by doing democracy. Mikva Challenge coined the term Action Civics in 2007 to describe our type of student-focused, project-based, experiential civic practice that can transform classrooms, schools, and whole communities.

Mikva develops cutting edge curricula to bring Action Civics alive in classrooms. Learn more about our Action Civics Curricula available for purchase.
