Virtual Spring Benefit & Youth Voice Campaign

January 13, 2020

Thank you to everyone who was able to join us for our Virtual Spring Benefit! Watch the video now on facebook.

As the challenging realities of COVID-19 continue to grow throughout the nation, it is clear that student voices and civic engagement are essential now more than ever.

In light of social distancing guidelines, Mikva Challenge must officially cancel our Annual Spring Benefit. Although we may not be able to meet in person, we invite you to continue to create space in the coming weeks to celebrate youth changemakers, civic action and leadership with us. In lieu of a physical Spring Benefit this year, Mikva Challenge will hold the Virtual Spring Benefit on Thursday, May 28, 2020 at 5:30pm, and it will be filled with the best component of Mikva’s event – Youth Voice!

As we lead up to the event, through our Youth Voice Campaign we will be sharing stories with you of our powerful youth leaders and how Mikva Challenge is supporting our youth financially and emotionally, while providing the space needed to amplify youth voices. Many students have shared concerns on how COVID-19 is impacting the city, their families, their income and their education.

Your commitment is crucial. Although the event will be virtual, the impact of your support is real.

Please donate now or Sponsor our event to ensure that we can continue to support youth voice and agency at this critical moment. Please contact lakesha@mikvachallenge.org for more information.
